Our Story

A Taste of Bengal,
with Open Arms

At Amantran Dhaba, every meal is an invitation — আমন্ত্রণ means exactly that. We prepare exquisite Bengali Thalis and rich North Indian dishes with special care, without compromising on taste or quality.

Located at Moinagadi, Barasat, our roadside dhaba brings the soul of Bengali home cooking to every plate, with the warmth of outdoor seating and the joy of a shared table.
